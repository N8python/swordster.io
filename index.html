Welcome to the exciting io game, swordster.io! Unlike other io games, swordster is a single player dungeon adventure. Start with a sword and basic spells, and climb your way to the top with laser blasters and powerful magic!

Insructions: Look at the middle of the screen. The square in the middle is the dungeon. All the action happens there. You fight enemies, and try to get to the bottom right (the treasure chest) The player is the little green guy in the corner. Move your player around with the arrow keys. Use space to use your selected weapon. Cast your spells with the number keys. Each new spell you learn corresponds to the number keys. You can check info about spells in the spell block. In the spell block, you'll see 10 different blocks. The ones that don't have question marks in them indicate "unlocked spells". Hover over an unlocked spell to see data on it. In addition, you can buy new spells with coins. Above the spell block you'll find the status bar which displays your HP and Mana bar. Mana is what you use to cast your spells. In addition, you can upgrade your HP and Mana in the status bar. Finally, on the left, you will find the weapons bar. Here, you can view satistics about all the weapons in the game. Scroll through the weapons with the up and down arrow keys. You can also buy weapons here. You also will see your coin count here. Coins are the way you buy stuff in the game. Happy Playing!
